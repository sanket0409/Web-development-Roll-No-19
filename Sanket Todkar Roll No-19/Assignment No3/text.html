Web development is the process of creating and maintaining websites or web applications. It involves a variety of tasks, including web design, coding, content creation, and database management, to build a fully functional online presence. Web developers use programming languages like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and server-side languages like PHP or Python to bring a website to life. The goal is to ensure a seamless, interactive, and responsive user experience across different devices and platforms. Web development is an essential field that supports businesses, organizations, and individuals in reaching their audience and achieving their goals online.
